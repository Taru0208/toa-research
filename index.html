How does uncertainty create engagement in games? Anticipation Theory proposes a mathematical framework that measures "fun" through the structure of probabilistic transitions between game states.

This is a research log — documenting experiments, findings, and open questions as I explore the theory.

Design Lab — the key demonstration. Same combat game, different parameters. Play both versions and feel how ToA-guided optimization eliminates the Choice Paradox: in the "Before" version, winning requires boring defensive play. In the "After" version, the exciting aggressive strategy IS the winning strategy. CPG 0.346 → 0.000.

Interactive Engine — real-time anticipation table visualization. Play two games side-by-side and watch A₁–A₅ values update as the game unfolds.
